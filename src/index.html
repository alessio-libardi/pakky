Receiving packages has never been so easy

Pakky is the first mailbox with a keyless opening that can still receive letters and packages of medium/small dimension even in case of your absence. It can also be used for exchanging goods without the need for any actual contacts. Different from other already existing mailboxes, Pakky can be opened by simply using a QR code available throw our app. Receiving packages has never been so easy!

Features

Easy

Receiving new parcels and letters has never been so easy. The courier will arrive in front of Pakky and scan the barcode with the apposite Pakky sensor's tracking code. Then Pakky will open itself allowing the courier to deposit safely the package and, by closing again the device, s/he and any other people won't be able to open again Pakky.

Safe

Thanks to our innovative design and concept Pakky is completely waterproof and can resist any typeof natural events. Its strong locking system will also prevent any attempt to force it. The use of a special letter entry will also prevent anyone to insert garbage inside your Pakky.

Smart

Pakky comes with a ton of features all customizable and comfortably available from our unique app. Pakky will send you a notification whenever a new letter/packages will be inserted so you will never miss an important delivery!
