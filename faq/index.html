F.A.Q.

  • What is the weight of Pakky?

    The weight of Pakky is 2,5 kg.

  • Can the Pakky be stolen?

    No. We use a classic fixation: a resistance force of 22kg which prevents pakky from being stolen.

  • How I need to open it when I have a package?

    When you have a package, you open Pakky with a QR code generated by your phone application. You just need to show this code to the Pakky and it will be open by itself.

  • How I will be sure that courier put the package inside?

    To be sure that the courier put the package inside and doesn’t take it back, we have the feedback from the piezoelectric weight sensor that will detect a minimal 20g package at least.

  • From which material Pakky is made?

    Pakky is made of 0,7mm electrogalvanized steel which prevents water corrosion.

  • Can I give my QR-code for somebody else?

    Yes, you can. You can give permanent access to the Pakky sharing your account in the APP or you can give the one-off QR-code for your friend or family, which will not work after one opening.
